## Daily Routine Template

1. **Warm-up** (15 min): Review 5-10 flashcards
2. **Deep work** (90 min): Exercises (SQL or Python)
3. **Break** (15 min)
4. **Practice** (60 min): System design or mock interview
5. **Review** (30 min): Cheatsheet, weak areas, progress check
